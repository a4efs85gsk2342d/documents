# Dr. Fukuhara Tomomi

The surgeon identified as "Fukuhara T." on the Niigata University orthopedic trauma team is **普久原 朝海 (Fukuhara Tomomi)**, an Assistant Professor at the Advanced Disaster Medical and Emergency Critical Care Center of Niigata University Medical and Dental Hospital.

He is one of Japan's foremost pelvic fracture surgeons, co-editor of the country's first comprehensive surgical textbook on pelvic fractures, and has been at Niigata University since May 2011.

## Subspecialty Interests and Pelvic Fracture Expertise

Fukuhara's [researchmap profile](https://researchmap.jp/TomomiFukuhara) lists his research keywords as:

- **骨盤輪骨折** (pelvic ring fractures)
- **寛骨臼骨折** (acetabular fractures)
- **整形外傷** (orthopedic trauma)
- **整形外科** (orthopedic surgery)

### Textbook editorship

His most significant academic achievement is serving as **co-editor** of:

> **骨盤骨折：寛骨臼骨折・骨盤輪骨折の手術手技**
> *(Pelvic Fractures: Surgical Techniques for Acetabular and Pelvic Ring Fractures)*
> Medica Shuppan, 2022 · ISBN: 978-4-8404-7887-8
> [Publisher listing (Medica)](https://store.medica.co.jp/item/402460060) · [m2plus e-book listing](https://www.m2plus.com/content/11199)

This is described as Japan's first comprehensive textbook dedicated to pelvic fracture surgery, complete with web-based surgical videos. Co-editor Mogami Atsuhiko described Fukuhara as one of the surgeons **"most active in the pelvic fracture field in Japan today."**

### Technical expertise

His specific techniques include:

- **Modified Subcristal external fixation** for pelvic ring fractures (refined and presented multiple times)
- **Percutaneous sacral screw fixation**
- **Modified Stoppa approach** for acetabular fractures (covered in a two-part invited serial publication)
- **Preperitoneal pelvic packing (PPP)**
- **Posterior plate fixation** for sacral fractures
- **Masquelet technique** for bone defects
- **24 book chapters** across major Japanese orthopedic texts covering these techniques

> **Sources:** [Medica Shuppan textbook listing](https://store.medica.co.jp/item/402460060) · [m2plus textbook listing](https://www.m2plus.com/content/11199) · [J-GLOBAL publication list](https://jglobal.jst.go.jp/detail?JGLOBAL_ID=202101020345248670)

## Surname Note: 普久原 ≠ 福原

The surname **普久原** is of [Okinawan origin](https://ja.wikipedia.org/wiki/%E6%99%AE%E4%B9%85%E5%8E%9F) and is distinct from the far more common mainland Japanese surname 福原. Both romanize to "Fukuhara" in standard Hepburn romanization, but they are written with entirely different kanji. This is the key detail that makes Dr. Fukuhara Tomomi difficult to locate through standard Japanese-language database searches.

## Conclusion

Dr. Fukuhara Tomomi (普久原朝海) is not merely a trauma team member but a **nationally recognized authority on pelvic fracture surgery in Japan**. His editorial role on Japan's definitive pelvic fracture surgery textbook, combined with his committee positions at JSFR and JOA, 24 book chapters primarily on pelvic and acetabular techniques, and over 49 conference presentations, places him squarely among the country's top orthopedic trauma specialists. His work at Niigata University's Advanced Disaster Medical and Emergency Critical Care Center — embedded within an orthopedic department that performs one of the highest volumes of pelvic fracture surgery in Japan — has allowed him to develop deep expertise in external fixation techniques (Modified Subcristal method), acetabular surgical approaches (Modified Stoppa), sacral fracture fixation, and preperitoneal pelvic packing: the full spectrum of pelvic and acetabular fracture management.
